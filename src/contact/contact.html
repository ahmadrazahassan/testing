Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gooomly?

Gooomly is a marketplace for creators to showcase and sell their high-quality digital products, from UI kits to 3D models.

How do I become a creator?

You can become a creator by signing up for one of our pricing plans on the homepage. Once you've signed up, you'll get access to your creator dashboard to start uploading products.

What are the fees for selling on the marketplace?

Our fees depend on the pricing plan you choose. The Basic plan has a higher transaction fee, while the Pro and Enterprise plans offer lower fees and more features. Please refer to the pricing section on our homepage for more details.
